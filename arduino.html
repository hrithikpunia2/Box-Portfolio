Using Arduino

Check out this video!

For this assignment, I combined the proximity sensor, RGB LED, Buzzer & Arduino. In this application, when the sensor senses any object within 10 cm, the RGB LED turns red and the buzzer starts to buzz. If not in the 10 cm range then the RGB LED stays green.
